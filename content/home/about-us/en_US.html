About us

As the Department for International Trade, we help overseas companies locate and grow in the UK.

We also help businesses export and grow into global markets.

Our global team of staff based in British Embassies, High Commissions or Consulates help companies to locate or invest in the UK.

This international network can:

  1. Build connections with your sectors ecosystem and supply chain
  2. Assist with visa applications and other immigration issues
  3. Help you to find grants and incentives, including geographical clusters
  4. Assist with hiring staff through salary benchmarking, recruitment, retention and training
  5. Provide insight on locations and market conditions based on your needs
